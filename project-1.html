Vrygrond Community Map

A web application featuring a search function and interactive map for finding non-profit organizations and services.

Live Link

Project Overview

I was able to work on a seven week global internship experience for the Butterfly Art Project and the Vrygrond Community Trust. I worked on a small team as the lead frontend engineer and full stack developer. Our team built a MERN Stack application following the Agile framework. We built a community map consisting of non-profit organizations for the people of Vrygrond to find services that they require. Vrygrond is an underdeveloped community so we focused on using open source and low data components to keep cost to a minimum. It was an amazing experience applying what I learned in the classroom and building a web application that is currently still being used.

Special thanks to Dr. Sanethia Thomas, Ekin Atay, and Ping Neo for making my global internship possible and overseeing our project.
